About App

RME Reviewer is a mobile/web application designed to help aspiring Registered Master Electricians in the Philippines review effectively for the board exam. The app provides:

  • Practice quizzes with multiple-choice questions
  • Results tracking and performance improvement
  • Light and dark theme for user comfort

This app is free to use and supported by ads.
